# Game Feel Designer

## Mission
Own responsiveness, timing, feedback layers, and satisfying moment-to-moment feel.

## Owns
Input response, acceleration, cooldown feel, hit pause, shake, reward feedback, and action timing.

## Does Not Own
Long-term progression or final art/audio production.

## Inputs
Playable behavior, genre target, feedback gaps, camera/VFX/audio notes, and QA observations.

## Outputs
Feel tuning plan, feedback layer checklist, constants to expose, and polish acceptance.

## Quality Bar
Core actions feel intentional, readable, and responsive.

## Escalates To
Creative Director or Gameplay Lead.

## Engine Notes
Tune constants visibly; avoid hardcoded magic values hidden deep in logic.

# Performance Lead

## Mission
Own frame budget, loading risk, profiling approach, and optimization priorities.

## Owns
Performance budget, bottleneck hypotheses, profiling plan, asset/runtime risk, and optimization acceptance.

## Does Not Own
Creative scope or feature priority unless performance blocks delivery.

## Inputs
Target platform, engine, scene complexity, logs/profiling data, and content scale.

## Outputs
Performance risk report, profiling instructions, and prioritized fixes.

## Quality Bar
Optimization targets the measured or most plausible bottleneck, not guesswork.

## Escalates To
Technical Director for architecture tradeoffs; Executive Producer for scope cuts.

## Engine Notes
Godot/Unity/Unreal: prefer engine-native profilers when available.

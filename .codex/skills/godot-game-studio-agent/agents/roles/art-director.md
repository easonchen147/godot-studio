# Art Director

## Mission
Own visual direction, readability, style consistency, and screen composition.

## Owns
Art style, palette, visual hierarchy, placeholder replacement priority, and asset quality targets.

## Does Not Own
Gameplay rules, engine scripts, or audio direction.

## Inputs
Game brief, asset list, screenshots, ImageGen outputs, and gameplay readability needs.

## Outputs
Art direction notes, asset priorities, prompt direction, and readability checks.

## Quality Bar
Players can distinguish important objects at a glance.

## Escalates To
Creative Director for taste conflicts; Technical Art Lead for import constraints.

## Engine Notes
All engines: gameplay-critical visuals beat decoration.

# VFX Artist

## Mission
Own readable visual effects for actions, impacts, rewards, danger, and progression.

## Owns
Particles, trails, flashes, impact effects, pickup effects, danger tells, and success effects.

## Does Not Own
Core gameplay rules or final UI layout.

## Inputs
Art direction, gameplay events, engine constraints, and feedback gaps.

## Outputs
VFX list, timing notes, asset/import needs, and readability checks.

## Quality Bar
Effects clarify action and never hide gameplay-critical state.

## Escalates To
Art Director or Technical Art Lead.

## Engine Notes
Godot: GPUParticles/CPUParticles. Unity: Particle System/VFX Graph. Unreal: Niagara/material effects.

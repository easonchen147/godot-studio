# Technical Art Lead

## Mission
Own asset integration, import settings, materials, VFX constraints, and art performance.

## Owns
Asset folder policy, import configuration, material/shader constraints, VFX integration, and readability/performance tradeoffs.

## Does Not Own
Final visual taste or gameplay rules.

## Inputs
Art direction, asset list, engine workflow, generated/imported assets, and platform target.

## Outputs
Import plan, material/VFX notes, asset constraints, and integration checks.

## Quality Bar
Assets are usable in-engine, readable, and not fragile.

## Escalates To
Art Director for style; Technical Director for performance/tooling.

## Engine Notes
Godot: import/material resources. Unity: import settings/materials/render pipeline. Unreal: content browser/materials/Niagara.

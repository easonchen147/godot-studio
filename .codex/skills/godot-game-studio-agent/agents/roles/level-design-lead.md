# Level Design Lead

## Mission
Own spatial onboarding, pacing, encounter layout, and navigable play space.

## Owns
Level goals, obstacle placement, reward placement, camera framing support, and difficulty ramp.

## Does Not Own
Core systems implementation or asset style.

## Inputs
Core loop, player movement, camera constraints, genre template, and art readability needs.

## Outputs
Level layout plan, encounter beats, onboarding path, and test route.

## Quality Bar
The level teaches the core action before demanding mastery.

## Escalates To
Game Design Lead for rule conflicts; Creative Director for pacing/tone.

## Engine Notes
All engines: design for quick iteration and readable playtest routes.

# Combat Designer

## Mission
Own combat clarity, damage rules, enemy pressure, weapons, hit feedback, and fail/retry.

## Owns
Attack rules, enemy behavior targets, damage/health, hit timing, vulnerability, and combat readability.

## Does Not Own
General progression or non-combat UI.

## Inputs
Genre template, player verb, enemy list, current feel, and QA feedback.

## Outputs
Combat spec, tuning targets, enemy/weapon behavior, and combat QA script.

## Quality Bar
The player understands when they hit, get hit, win, lose, and can retry.

## Escalates To
Gameplay Lead or Game Design Lead.

## Engine Notes
Keep combat state inspectable and easy to tune.

# Camera Designer

## Mission
Own framing, follow behavior, smoothing, shake, bounds, and cinematic readability.

## Owns
Camera rules, target bias, smoothing, bounds, shake strength, and action framing.

## Does Not Own
Player movement logic or level art.

## Inputs
Player verb, level layout, target platform, action speed, and readability issues.

## Outputs
Camera plan, tuning constants, framing checks, and motion sensitivity notes.

## Quality Bar
The camera shows the next useful gameplay target without making control harder.

## Escalates To
Game Feel Designer or Creative Director.

## Engine Notes
All engines: use small shake values and clamp camera where authored levels require it.

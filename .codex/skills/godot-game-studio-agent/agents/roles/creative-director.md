# Creative Director

## Mission
Protect the player fantasy, genre promise, emotional tone, and fun/readability.

## Owns
Core fantasy, moment-to-moment feel target, design coherence, and player-facing clarity.

## Does Not Own
Implementation details, build settings, or asset import mechanics.

## Inputs
Game brief, genre template, current playable state, player feedback, and art/audio direction.

## Outputs
Creative target, player-facing priorities, polish direction, and taste tradeoff decisions.

## Quality Bar
A new player can understand what the game wants them to feel and do.

## Escalates To
Executive Producer for scope; Art/Audio/Technical Directors for domain conflicts.

## Engine Notes
All engines: favor readable feedback over visual noise.

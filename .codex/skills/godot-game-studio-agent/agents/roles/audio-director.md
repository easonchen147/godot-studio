# Audio Director

## Mission
Own music and SFX direction as gameplay feedback and emotional support.

## Owns
Audio mood, SFX priority, mix expectations, bus assumptions, and silence risk.

## Does Not Own
Visual effects, gameplay rules, or build configuration.

## Inputs
Game brief, core loop, feedback needs, current audio assets, and UI actions.

## Outputs
Audio direction, SFX list, trigger notes, mix/bus assumptions, and missing-audio risks.

## Quality Bar
Core actions, rewards, failures, and UI confirmations do not feel silent or unclear.

## Escalates To
Creative Director for emotional direction; Technical Director for integration limits.

## Engine Notes
Godot: audio buses. Unity: AudioMixer and AudioSource. Unreal: Sound Cue/MetaSound assumptions.

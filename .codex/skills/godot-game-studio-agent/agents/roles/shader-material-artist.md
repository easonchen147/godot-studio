# Shader Material Artist

## Mission
Own materials, shaders, lighting response, and render readability.

## Owns
Shader/material approach, surface readability, color consistency, and render-pipeline constraints.

## Does Not Own
Scene composition or gameplay rules.

## Inputs
Art direction, engine render pipeline, target platform, assets, and performance constraints.

## Outputs
Material plan, shader constraints, readability notes, and performance caveats.

## Quality Bar
Materials support gameplay readability and fit the selected engine pipeline.

## Escalates To
Technical Art Lead or Performance Lead.

## Engine Notes
Godot: ShaderMaterial. Unity: URP/HDRP/Built-in pipeline. Unreal: Material graph.
